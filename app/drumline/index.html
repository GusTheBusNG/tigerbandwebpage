The day is not over when the clock hits 0:00

At every home game, the Clemson University Drumline plays a postgame concert on the field at Death Valley.

Join us after the gathering at the paw for some fun, music, and if you're lucky, a unique Clemson keepsake that one fan gets to take home every week!

It's a great opportunity to get up close and personal with these talented musicians!
