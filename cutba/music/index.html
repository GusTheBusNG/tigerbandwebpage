Alumni Band Drumline Cadence Sequence

OPTION A (For when we have all instruments present with a minimum of 3 bass drums):

  1. Taps…
  2. Tiger March
  3. Roll off
  4. Tiger Rag
  5. Go Tigers Cheer
  6. Bananas
  7. Taps…
  8. Toe Jam
    9. (Repeat All)

OPTION B (For when we are running short on instruments or if we have less than 3 basses, we eliminate Toe Jam from the sequence):

  1. Taps…
  2. Tiger March
  3. Roll off
  4. Tiger Rag
  5. Go Tigers Cheer
  6. Bananas
    7. (Repeat All)
